Sifter for webpages

Highlight and save paragraphs in bookmarks
with different colors
  • Use Alt+Shift+Y to highlight paragraphs in yellow
  • Use Alt+Shift+R to highlight paragraphs in red
  • Use Alt+Shift+G to highlight paragraphs in green
  • Use Alt+Shift+B to highlight paragraph in blue
Search or edit existing paragraphs via
Revisit URL to get the highlighted paragraphs
and export it via email.
